Explorer's Story

Explorer was created by Sahara Smith because she realized how difficult it was to not only plan a trip, but also remember important information while on it. Explorer allows its users to plan, collaborate, and thoroughly enjoy each second of their trip. From a solo trip to a work trip, EXPLORE can help!
